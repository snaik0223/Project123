FACTS ABOUT THE FAVORiTE TV STARS

Shawn Hunter

Shawn Patrick Hunter is the deuteragonist of Boy Meets World and Cory's best friend. His rebellious attitude and popularity is often used as a foil to Cory's awkwardness and neurotics.

Cory Matthews

Cornelius "Cory" A. Matthews is the main protagonist and title character of Boy Meets World. Cory is introduced as a witty, sports fan with average interests but later he becomes a neurotic, but funny young man with a strong moral code. Throughout the series, we learn that the most important things to Cory are his love for his family, his best friend Shawn, and his girlfriend and future wife Topanga.

Topanga Lawrence

Topanga Lawrence-Matthews is the main love interest to the protagonist in Boy Meets World. She had a hippie, eccentric childhood and was ostracized in middle school. After puberty, she got rid of her Granola Girl image for the most part, though still retained her sincerity, ideals, and sharp wit. She loved Cory since they were very young and despite their ups and downs, they ended up together in the end. She's considered a bit of a know-it-all and competitive but she's protective and loyal to her friends.
